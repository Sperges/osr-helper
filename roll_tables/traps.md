# Trap
- Mechanism: `Mechanism`, Consequence: `Consequence`, Tell: `Tell`

## Mechanism
- tripwire
- weigth difference
- false object or mechanism
- removal or placement
- push or pull
- sound or disturbance

## Consequence
- displacement
- cutting or piercing
- crushing or paralyzing
- poison or water
- terrain destruction
- fire or alarm

# Tell
- too old or too new
- juts or recedes
- violates a pattern
- previous misfortune
- strange material or damage
- conspicuous
